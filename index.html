Why SwypeNest exists

The systems we have
still work like it's 2005.

I built SwypeNest because finding a home in Namibia is still a frustrating, time-consuming process that hasn't kept up with how we live. You scroll through Facebook Marketplace mixed with unrelated posts. You call agents who don't answer. You check newspapers. You hear about places through word of mouth and hope they're still available. It shouldn't be this hard.

My goal is simple: nobody in Namibia should spend more than an hour finding a home that works for them. And once they've found it, the process of managing that tenancy — payments, maintenance, communication — should be just as seamless. That's where we're going.

Africa's property market is one of the fastest-growing in the world. The tools being built for it should reflect that. SwypeNest is the start of something bigger — a platform that brings automation and intelligence to property management across the region, starting right here in Windhoek.
